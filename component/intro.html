Introduction

Synergic experience, like-minded thinking and common vision of “Outreaching Professional Skills for Positive Contribution in the Development Sphere and Enhancing Skills of Youth”, motivated a group of professional colleagues and university mates, to organize a self-sustainable engineering firm with the name of PROGEN Services in October 2022. Currently PROGEN Services is a recognized and qualified Engineering Consulting Firm, registered with Pakistan Engineering Council under Registration No. CONSULT/2482 and offers a broad range of professional, technical and specialized Civil, Electrical and Architectural services. It has organized a qualified team of planners, designers, construction and technical advisors having skills in planning and development of all type infrastructure comprising but not limited to rural and urban roads, drains, housing, commercial and industrial buildings along with community social infrastructure development.

The strategic goal is to make a positive difference in the development circle to contribute in the global Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) 2030 and Pakistan 2025 one Nation. Concerning SDGs, our main goal is also community development to involve all the stake holders in line, to up standards community livelihood in all components of SDG.

One Vision through offering innovative technical solutions to both the public and private development sectors, communities and national and international donor organizations in Pakistan. PROGEN Services claims that clients would be desirous to work with it, the primary being offering sustainable solutions to address their strategic and operational challenges in a more professional, qualitative, quantitative and cost-effective manner.

The cumulative breadth of experience of the professionals associated with PROGEN is over 25 years, which encourages PROGEN to take on more complex and challenging projects and ensure the client’s trust by exercising continuity, efficiency and transparency.

So, our value is that we are committed to the sustainability of communities, our clients businesses and the environments in which we live and work.
